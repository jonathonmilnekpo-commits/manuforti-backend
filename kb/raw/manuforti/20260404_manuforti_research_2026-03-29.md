# Manu Forti Sales Automation Research

**Date:** 2026-03-29  
**Research Focus:** AI Sales Automation & B2B Lead Generation  
**Areas Covered:** LinkedIn Outreach, Cold Email Automation, B2B Lead Intelligence

---

## 1. APOLLO.IO — B2B Lead Database + Outreach

**What it is:** All-in-one sales intelligence platform combining 275M+ contact database with AI-powered outreach tools.

**Free tier:** 50 email credits/month + lead search + email templates  
**Paid:** From ~$59/month  
**Trial:** 14-day free trial with 50 credits + 5 mobile credits

| Feature | Use Case for Manu Forti |
|---------|------------------------|
| Advanced filtering | Target procurement leaders by title, company size, industry |
| Email sequencing | Automated nurture campaigns for Product 1 prospects |
| CRM integration | Sync leads to your existing stack |
| Intent data | Find companies actively researching procurement solutions |

**Link:** <https://apollo.io/pricing>

---

## 2. INSTANTLY.AI — Cold Email Automation

**What it is:** Specialized cold email platform with unlimited mailboxes, AI warmup, and deliverability focus.

**Free tier:** 14-day free trial available  
**Paid:** From $37/month (unlimited email accounts + warmup)

| Feature | Use Case for Manu Forti |
|---------|------------------------|
| Unlimited mailboxes | Scale without per-seat pricing |
| AI warmup pool (200K+ accounts) | Land in primary inbox, not spam |
| B2B lead database | Built-in 160M+ contacts |
| Inbox rotation | Distribute sends across accounts |
| AI sequence generator | Auto-create email variants |

**Link:** <https://instantly.ai/pricing>

---

## 3. N8N — Open Source Workflow Automation

**What it is:** Free, self-hosted workflow automation (like Zapier but open source + code-friendly).

**Cost:** Free (self-hosted) / Cloud from $20/month  
**License:** Fair-code open source

| Feature | Use Case for Manu Forti |
|---------|------------------------|
| 400+ integrations | Connect LinkedIn, email, CRM, Slack |
| AI workflow nodes | GPT-4 powered lead qualification |
| Self-hosted option | Full data control for enterprise clients |
| JavaScript/Python support | Custom logic for procurement-specific workflows |
| Template library | 280+ pre-built workflows including sales outreach |

**Sales Workflow Example:**
1. Apollo trigger → New procurement lead saved
2. GPT-4 node → Personalize outreach message
3. Send email → Via Gmail/Outlook node
4. Slack alert → Notify when lead responds

**Link:** <https://n8n.io/workflows/> (browse AI + Sales templates)

## QUICK COMPARISON

| Tool | Best For | Cost | Setup |
|------|----------|------|-------|
| Apollo.io | Lead discovery + basic outreach | Free tier → $59/mo | 5 min |
| Instantly.ai | High-volume cold email campaigns | $37/mo unlimited | 15 min |
| n8n | Custom automation + data control | Free self-hosted | 1-2 hours |

## RECOMMENDATION

**Phase 1 (Immediate):** Apollo free tier — start building prospect lists for Product 1 soft launch

**Phase 2 (Scaling):** Instantly.ai — when ready for systematic cold outreach at volume

**Phase 3 (Advanced):** n8n — when you need custom workflows connecting multiple tools or handling sensitive procurement data
