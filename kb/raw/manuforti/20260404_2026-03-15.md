**TOPSIS Structure:**
- Input: Options (rows) × Criteria (columns) scoring matrix (1-10)
- Step 1: Normalized decision matrix
- Step 2: Weighted normalized matrix
- Step 3: Positive Ideal Solution (PIS) — best score per criterion
- Step 4: Negative Ideal Solution (NIS) — worst score per criterion
- Step 5: Distance from PIS (Euclidean)
- Step 6: Distance from NIS (Euclidean)
- Step 7: TOPSIS score = distance NIS / (distance PIS + distance NIS)
- Output: Ranked options with scores (0-1, higher = better)
