5. **Token Awareness Trigger** — Generating >5k tokens for a one-line response = automatic pause and reassess.

6. **Reference the Actual File** — HEARTBEAT.md was in context. Should have looked at what it actually says instead of inventing fictional formulas.

**Bottom line:** User pays for competence, not creativity. When in doubt, do the boring thing correctly.
