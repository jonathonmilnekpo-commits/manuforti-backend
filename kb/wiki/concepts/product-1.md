# Product 1

**Type:** Product
**Category:** Manu Forti
**First Seen:** 2026-02-22

## Definition

Product 1 is Manu Forti's AI-powered Supplier Analysis Report — a 9-slide executive evaluation deck providing comprehensive supplier due diligence for procurement professionals.

## Summary

Product 1 delivers professional-grade supplier intelligence in 24-48 hours. Each report includes financial health assessment, risk scoring, market position analysis, ESG evaluation, and strategic recommendations. Reports follow the canonical v15 template with Wood Mackenzie styling.

The product targets category managers and procurement directors evaluating suppliers for strategic contracts. Priced at €249-€499 per report depending on tier (Standard/Premium/Enterprise).

## Report Structure

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Title (supplier name, date) |
| 2 | Executive Summary (risk gauge, key metrics) |
| 3 | Recommendation (approve/reject/conditional) |
| 4 | Supplier Profile (org chart, ownership) |
| 5 | Financial Health (3-year trend, EBITDA) |
| 6 | Market Position (vs competitors) |
| 7 | Operational Capability (timeline, facilities) |
| 8 | Commercial Intelligence (radar chart) |
| 9 | ESG Assessment (3-pillar rating) |

## Pipeline Agents

1. **Vetter** — Validates order parameters and source documents
2. **Researcher** — Gathers financial data, market intel, risk indicators
3. **Venture** — Generates report with charts, tables, analysis
4. **Validator** — Quality check against canonical template (100/100)
5. **Aiden** — Final review and delivery
