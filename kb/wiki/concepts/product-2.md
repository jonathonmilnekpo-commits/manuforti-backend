# Product 2

**Type:** Product
**Category:** Manu Forti
**First Seen:** 2026-03-12

## Definition

Product 2 is the Procurement Leadership Academy — a comprehensive training program teaching procurement teams to design, build, and deploy AI agents for procurement operations.

## Summary

The Academy transforms procurement teams into AI-native operators through a structured 8-week curriculum covering AI strategy, agent architecture, change management, and advanced applications. Delivered in multiple formats (Executive Workshop, Practitioner Certification, Technical Deep-Dive, Enterprise Custom).

The Academy fills the gap between generic AI training and procurement-specific implementation. Participants learn to build functional agents using OpenClaw, create agent workflows, and manage human-AI collaboration. Priced at €2,000-€12,000 depending on format.

## Curriculum Structure

### Pillar 1: AI Strategy for Procurement (Weeks 1-2)
- AI landscape for procurement
- Organizational readiness assessment
- Business case development
- 12-month roadmap creation
- **Deliverable:** Personalized AI readiness assessment + roadmap

### Pillar 2: Agent Architecture & Implementation (Weeks 3-5)
- Agent capabilities and limitations
- Procurement use case mapping
- Workflow design patterns
- Technical architecture (non-technical)
- **Deliverable:** Designed agent workflow + functional prototype

### Pillar 3: Change Management & Adoption (Weeks 6-7)
- Redefining procurement roles
- Human-AI interface management
- Training & enablement programs
- Stakeholder management
- **Deliverable:** Change management plan + upskilling framework

### Pillar 4: Advanced Applications (Week 8)
- Autonomous sourcing agents
- Predictive risk intelligence
- Natural language contract analysis
- Real-time market intelligence
- **Deliverable:** Advanced use case prioritization matrix

## Delivery Formats

| Format | Duration | Target | Price |
