# Product 3

**Type:** Product
**Category:** Manu Forti
**First Seen:** 2026-03-29

## Definition

Product 3 is Manu Forti's Media Monitoring Service — continuous surveillance of supplier and competitor media coverage with sentiment analysis, risk alerts, and strategic intelligence.

## Summary

Media Monitoring provides ongoing visibility into supplier reputation, market sentiment, and emerging risks between deep-dive analyses. The service tracks traditional news, social media, regulatory filings, and industry publications, delivering weekly summaries or monthly comprehensive reports.

The product complements Product 1 by providing early warning signals and trend tracking. Three tiers accommodate different needs: Monitor (€35/mo), Alert (€105/mo), and Enterprise (custom). Reports include sentiment scoring, competitive benchmarking, ESG controversy detection, and strategic recommendations.

## Monitoring Scope

1. Company announcements & press releases
2. Executive statements & interviews
3. Financial results & earnings
4. Product launches & updates
5. Partnerships & acquisitions
6. Legal & regulatory issues
7. Industry position & market sentiment
8. Competitor mentions

## Platform Coverage

### Traditional News (Tier 1: 2.5x weight)
- Reuters, Bloomberg, Financial Times
- Wall Street Journal, industry publications

### Social Media
- X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit, forums

### Regulatory/Official
- Press releases, SEC filings
- Government announcements

## Report Components

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Executive Summary | Risk score, alert summary, sentiment trajectory |
| Critical Findings | URGENT/HIGH/MEDIUM flagged items |
| Trend Analysis | 30-day sentiment chart, volume analysis |
| Traditional Media | Article table with sentiment |
| Social Media | Platform breakdown, influencer mentions |
| ESG & Regulatory | Controversies, compliance status |
| Competitive Intelligence | Competitor mentions, market positioning |
| Strategic Recommendations | Immediate/short-term/long-term actions |

## Sentiment Scoring

- 40% headline sentiment
- 35% body content sentiment
- 15% source credibility
- 10% engagement metrics

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Frequency | Suppliers | Features |
|------|-------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Monitor | €35/mo | Weekly email | 10 | Traditional media only |
| Alert | €105/mo | Monthly full report | 25 | + Social media, alerts |
| Enterprise | Custom | Weekly reports | Unlimited | + API, WhatsApp alerts, dashboards |

## Pipeline Agents

1. **Monitor** — Collects data from all sources continuously
2. **Analyzer** — Processes sentiment, identifies trends
3. **Reporter** — Generates formatted reports
4. **Validator** — Quality checks and fact-checks
5. **Aiden** — Final review and delivery
