# Product 3: Media Monitoring Service

**Type:** Product Documentation
**Category:** Manu Forti
**First Seen:** 2026-03-29

## Overview

Product 3 provides continuous media surveillance of suppliers and competitors, delivering early warning signals and strategic intelligence between deep-dive analyses.

## Value Proposition

- **Early Warning:** Detect issues before they become crises
- **Continuous Coverage:** 24/7 monitoring vs point-in-time reports
- **Actionable Intelligence:** Sentiment trends, risk alerts, competitive moves
- **Affordable Entry:** €35/mo makes monitoring accessible to all

## Target Customer

- Category Managers tracking supplier reputation
- Risk officers monitoring counterparty exposure
- Competitive intelligence professionals
- Investor relations teams

## Monitoring Scope

1. **Company Announcements** — Press releases, investor communications
2. **Executive Statements** — Interviews, speeches, social media
3. **Financial Results** — Earnings, guidance, analyst calls
4. **Product Launches** — New offerings, updates, recalls
5. **Partnerships** — M&A, joint ventures, strategic alliances
6. **Legal & Regulatory** — Lawsuits, fines, compliance issues
7. **Industry Position** — Market sentiment, analyst ratings
8. **Competitor Mentions** — Comparative coverage, competitive threats

## Platform Coverage

### Traditional News
- Reuters, Bloomberg, Financial Times (Tier 1: 2.5x weight)
- Wall Street Journal, industry publications
- Regional business press

### Social Media
- X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit
- Industry forums and discussion boards

### Regulatory/Official
- SEC filings, press releases
- Government announcements
- Court records

## Report Contents

### Executive Dashboard
- Real-time risk score (0-100)
- Critical alerts summary
- Sentiment trajectory
- Key metrics at a glance

### Critical Findings
- **URGENT:** Immediate notification required
- **HIGH:** Significant developments
- **MEDIUM:** Notable mentions
- **MONITOR:** Early signals

### Trend Analysis
- 30-day sentiment chart
- Volume analysis (article count trends)
- Source breakdown
- Geographic distribution

### Traditional Media Coverage
- Comprehensive article table
- Sentiment by source type
- Key headlines analysis

### Social Media Monitoring
- Platform-by-platform breakdown
- High-engagement posts
- Influencer mentions
- Hashtag tracking

### ESG & Regulatory
- Environmental controversies
- Social/license to operate issues
- Governance/legal proceedings
- Indigenous rights monitoring

### Competitive Intelligence
- Competitor mentions in same articles
- Market positioning shifts
- Industry trends affecting client

### Strategic Recommendations
- Immediate actions (next 7 days)
- Short-term priorities (30 days)
- Long-term strategic considerations

## Sentiment Scoring

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Headline | 40% | Emotional tone of headline |
| Body | 35% | Content sentiment analysis |
| Source | 15% | Credibility weighting |
| Engagement | 10% | Social amplification |

Scale: 0 (highly negative) to 100 (highly positive)

## ESG Red Flags (Auto-Alert)

- "lawsuit" + company name
- "license suspended"
- "environmental permit denied"
- "indigenous rights violation"
- "protest" + project name
- "blockade"
- "executive resignation" + controversy
- "regulatory investigation"
- "ESG rating downgrade"

## Operational Workflow

### Daily Operations
- Continuous automated data collection
- Real-time alert processing
- Duplicate detection and merging

### Weekly Rhythm
- Monday: Data aggregation begins
- Wednesday: Analysis and scoring
- Friday: Report generation
- Weekend: Quality review

### Monthly Production
- Comprehensive 30-day analysis
- Trend comparison vs previous month
- Strategic recommendations update

## Quality Standards

- 95%+ mention capture rate
- <2% false positive rate on alerts
- 24-hour maximum alert latency
- 100% source attribution
