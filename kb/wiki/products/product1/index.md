# Product 1: Supplier Analysis Reports

**Type:** Product Documentation
**Category:** Manu Forti
**First Seen:** 2026-02-22

## Overview

Product 1 delivers professional-grade supplier intelligence reports for procurement professionals. Each report is a 9-slide PowerPoint deck providing comprehensive due diligence on a single supplier, delivered within 24-48 hours.

## Value Proposition

- **Speed:** 24-48 hour turnaround vs weeks for traditional consulting
- **Quality:** Professional charts, risk gauges, market analysis
- **Price:** €249-499 vs €30-80k for McKinsey/Big 4 equivalent
- **Actionable:** Clear approve/reject/conditional recommendation

## Target Customer

- Category Managers evaluating strategic suppliers
- Procurement Directors conducting due diligence
- CFOs assessing counterparty risk
- Sustainability officers reviewing ESG compliance

## Report Contents

### Slide 1: Title
- Supplier name, report date, Manu Forti branding

### Slide 2: Executive Summary
- Risk score gauge (0-100 scale)
- Key metrics snapshot
- One-line recommendation

### Slide 3: Recommendation
- APPROVE / REJECT / CONDITIONAL APPROVAL
- Key conditions or concerns
- Commercial terms guidance

### Slide 4: Supplier Profile
- Parent company structure
- Geographic footprint
- Key facilities
- Executive leadership

### Slide 5: Financial Health
- 3-year revenue trend
- EBITDA margin analysis
- Debt/EBITDA ratio
- Cash position
- Forward guidance

### Slide 6: Market Position
- Competitive landscape bar chart
- Market share vs top 3-5 competitors
- Differentiation factors

### Slide 7: Operational Capability
- Strategic timeline (funding, capacity expansion)
- Manufacturing locations
- Certifications (ISO, industry-specific)

### Slide 8: Commercial Intelligence
- Radar chart (price, quality, delivery, innovation, service, ESG)
- Peer risk comparison
- Contract negotiation insights

### Slide 9: ESG Assessment
- Environmental rating
- Social rating
- Governance rating
- Controversy screening (16-point checklist)

## Delivery Process

1. **Order Placement** — Customer submits supplier name via website
2. **Vetter Review** — Validate inputs (15 min)
3. **Research Phase** — Gather financials, market data (2-4 hours)
4. **Report Generation** — Build charts, write analysis (2-3 hours)
5. **Validation** — Quality check against template (30 min)
6. **Delivery** — Email report to customer
