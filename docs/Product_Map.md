### What It Is
Professional 9-slide supplier evaluation reports combining AI analysis with procurement expertise.

### Target Customer
- Procurement managers evaluating new suppliers
- Category managers conducting market assessments
- Sourcing teams needing rapid due diligence

### Value Proposition
- **Speed:** 24-hour delivery vs weeks of manual research
- **Depth:** Financial, operational, geopolitical, ESG analysis
- **Quality:** Executive-ready format, actionable recommendations

### Pricing

| Standard | €99 | 24h | Single supplier check |
| Premium | €149 | 12h | Time-sensitive decisions |
| Enterprise | €499 | 6h | Strategic suppliers |

## Strategic Notes

### Why Product 2 (Academy) Makes Sense Now
1. **Product 1 proves capability** — We can build AI-powered procurement tools
2. **Customer demand** — Buyers will want to understand how to use AI themselves
3. **Higher margins** — Training €2K-10K vs reports €99-499
4. **Recurring revenue** — Certification renewals, ongoing programs
5. **Thought leadership** — Establishes Manu Forti as category leader

### Risks
- **Capacity:** Jonathon's time for content development
- **Market readiness:** Are procurement leaders ready for AI agents?
- **Competition:** Big consulting firms (McKinsey, BCG) could enter

### Mitigations
- Start with pilot, iterate based on feedback
- Leverage Product 1 success stories as proof points
- Move fast while market is nascent
