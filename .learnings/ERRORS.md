```markdown

What went wrong

Why it happened
```

## Log Entries

## 2026-03-11: Missed Critical ESG Issue in Media Monitoring Report

**Category:** knowledge
**Severity:** high

### Error Details
Created a media monitoring report for Boskalis that failed to include the Both ENDS legal action regarding the South Sulawesi sand extraction project. This is a significant ESG controversy that has been ongoing since 2017.

### Root Cause
Search queries focused on "financial results" and "contracts" but did not include ESG-specific terms like "lawsuit", "controversy", or "environmental". The Both ENDS legal action is a well-documented case that should have been caught.

### Resolution
Updated search strategy to always include ESG and legal terms. Added Both ENDS case to the report.

### Prevention
For all media monitoring reports, include these mandatory search terms:
- "[Company] lawsuit legal controversy"
- "[Company] ESG environmental social"
- "[Company] human rights"
- Check Business & Human Rights Resource Centre
- Check NGO sources (Both ENDS, Friends of the Earth, etc.)
