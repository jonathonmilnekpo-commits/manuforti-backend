## Tips for Jonathon

1. **Personalize every first message** — At minimum, reference their company or a recent post. Generic messages get ignored.

2. **Lead with value, not product** — Nobody cares about your features. They care about their problems.

3. **Follow up consistently** — 80% of responses come after the second or third message. Most people give up after one.

4. **Track everything** — Use the tracking sheet. Know who you've contacted, when, and what they said.

5. **Qualify hard on calls** — Not every prospect is a fit. Better to disqualify early than waste time on bad fits.

6. **Iterate based on data** — If connection rates are low, refine targeting. If response rates are low, refine messaging.

7. **Batch your work** — Research 10 prospects at once. Write 10 messages at once. Send in batches. More efficient than one-by-one.
