## Conversation Starters by Industry

### Renewable Energy (Scatec, OX2, Ørsted)

**Angle:** Rapid growth = supplier onboarding at scale

> "With the expansion in offshore wind, how are you handling supplier qualification at speed? We're seeing teams struggle to vet new suppliers fast enough to keep projects on track."

### Oil & Gas Services (Aker Solutions, Subsea 7)

**Angle:** Cost pressure + risk management

> "With the cost pressures in oil services, how are you balancing supplier cost reduction with operational risk? We're seeing procurement teams needing faster, deeper supplier intelligence."

### Industrial Manufacturing (Atlas Copco, SKF)

**Angle:** Supply chain resilience

> "Post-pandemic, how has your approach to supplier risk assessment changed? We're working with industrial companies to build faster supplier evaluation processes."

### Aluminium/Process Industry (Norsk Hydro)

**Angle:** ESG + supplier compliance

> "With increasing ESG scrutiny in aluminium, how are you evaluating supplier sustainability credentials? Our reports include ESG screening as standard."
