## Objection Handling

### "We're not ready for AI yet"
**Response:** "Most procurement teams aren't — that's exactly why the Academy starts with readiness assessment and roadmap development. You'll know exactly where you are and what steps to take, whether that's 90 days or 2 years. The goal isn't to rush you; it's to prepare you."

### "Our IT team handles technology"
**Response:** "IT implements technology; procurement leaders must define what agents should do and how they integrate with sourcing, contracting, and supplier management. The Academy gives you the vocabulary and frameworks to lead that conversation with IT, not defer to them."

### "We already use ChatGPT"
**Response:** "ChatGPT is a tool. Agents are systems that can autonomously execute procurement workflows. The Academy teaches you to move from individual productivity tools to organizational transformation — designing agents that work 24/7 on supplier risk monitoring, contract analysis, and market intelligence."

### "We don't have budget for training"
**Response:** "The Academy pays for itself through one avoided supplier risk event or one optimized sourcing process. More importantly, it positions your team to capture AI value before competitors do. What's the cost of being 12 months behind on AI adoption?"

### "We need to see ROI first"
**Response:** "The pilot Executive Workshop is designed for exactly this — a 2-day intensive that delivers a readiness assessment and 12-month roadmap. You'll know exactly what ROI to expect and how to achieve it before committing to broader implementation."

## Q&A for Sales Calls

**Q: Who is the ideal participant?**
A: CPOs, VPs, and Directors who have a mandate to explore AI but need a structured approach. Technical background not required — we cover what you need to know.

**Q: What's the time commitment?**
A: Executive Workshop is 2 full days. Practitioner Certification is 2 hours/week for 8 weeks plus implementation sprint. Technical Deep-Dive is 3 full days.

**Q: Do I need technical skills?**
A: No. The Academy is designed for procurement leaders, not developers. You'll learn enough to architect solutions and lead technical teams, but won't be coding (unless you choose the Technical Deep-Dive).

**Q: What if my organization isn't ready?**
A: The Academy includes readiness assessment and roadmap development. You'll know exactly what preparation is needed and how long it will take. Many graduates use the roadmap to build organizational readiness before implementation.

**Q: How is this different from hiring a consultant?**
A: Consultants deliver recommendations; the Academy builds your capability. You'll graduate able to design, implement, and scale procurement agents without ongoing external dependency.

**Q: What's the ROI?**
A: Typical ROI comes from: (1) automated processes saving FTE time, (2) risk monitoring preventing disruptions, (3) faster sourcing cycles, (4) improved negotiation outcomes. Most participants see payback within 6-12 months.

**Q: Can I get my team trained?**
A: Yes — we offer group discounts and can customize delivery for your organization. Enterprise packages include team-wide capability building.

**Q: What support do I get after the program?**
A: All programs include 3 months of post-program support via email/Slack. Alumni network provides ongoing peer support. Advanced packages include longer-term coaching.
