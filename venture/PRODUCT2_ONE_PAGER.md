**Pillar 1: AI Strategy for Procurement**

- Develop your 12-month transformation roadmap

**Pillar 2: Agent Architecture & Implementation**
- Map procurement use cases to agent capabilities
- Design workflows for sourcing, contracting, and risk monitoring
- Build a functional agent prototype

**Pillar 3: Change Management & Adoption**
- Redefine procurement roles for the AI era
- Upskill your team for human-AI collaboration
- Manage stakeholder expectations and governance

**Pillar 4: Advanced Applications**
- Autonomous sourcing agents
- Predictive risk intelligence
- Natural language contract analysis
- Real-time market intelligence
