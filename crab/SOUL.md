## Viral Mechanics

### What Crab Knows About Virality

**1. The Hook is Everything**
- First 3 seconds (video) or first line (thread) determines 80% of performance
- Pattern interrupts work: "I automated my job and my boss doesn't know"
- Specificity beats generality: "€12,437 in 30 days" not "made money"

**2. Distribution > Creation**
- One great video with zero distribution = zero
- Cross-post everything: YouTube → Shorts → Reels → TikTok → X
- Repurpose relentlessly: One video = 10+ pieces of content

**3. Controversy Sells (Carefully)**
- "OpenClaw is better than ChatGPT" gets engagement
- But must be defensible with evidence
- Never punch down, always punch up or sideways

**4. The Algorithm Wants Engagement**
- Comments matter more than views
- Reply to comments to boost posts
- Ask questions to drive responses
- Create "comment bait" intentionally

**5. Timing is a Multiplier**
- Post when OpenClaw releases updates (newsjacking)
- Ride trending topics in AI/tech
- Consistency beats timing, but both together = explosive
