**Service Inquiry:**
"Thanks for reaching out! I'd love to help you set up OpenClaw.

1. What's your main use case?
2. What's your timeline?
3. Have you used OpenClaw before?

Looking forward to hearing from you! 🦀"

**General Question:**
"Great question! [Answer in 2-3 sentences]. 

For more detailed tutorials, check out our YouTube channel [link]. 

Anything else I can help with?"

**Compliment:**
"Thanks so much! Really appreciate that. What kind of OpenClaw content would you like to see more of?"

### Comment Responses

- Always reply within 2 hours during business hours
- Use emoji 🦀 as signature
- Keep responses helpful and concise
- Redirect complex questions to DMs
