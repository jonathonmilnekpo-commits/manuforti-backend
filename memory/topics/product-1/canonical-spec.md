## 9 Slides (LOCKED)

1. **Title** — Supplier name, tagline, key stats
2. **Executive Summary** — Risk gauge (left, 7.2") + Supplier snapshot (right, 5.4")
3. **Recommendation** — Green/amber/red banner + conditions + risk summary
4. **Supplier Profile** — Org structure (left) + overview + leadership (right)
5. **Financial Health** — Dual-axis chart (left, 7.5") + 10-metrics panel (right, 4.8")
6. **Market Position** — Horizontal bar chart (left, 7.5") + competitive context (right, 4.8")
7. **Operational + Risk** — Timeline (left, 7.0") + 2x2 matrix (right, 5.5") + risk table (bottom)
8. **Commercial Intelligence** — Radar chart (left, 6.0") + peer risk (top-right) + terms (bottom-right)
9. **ESG Assessment** — E/S/G columns + controversy screening + rating + conditions

## Required Financial Metrics (Slide 5)

Must include in right-hand panel:
1. Revenue (latest year + YoY %)
2. EBITDA (amount + margin %)
3. Net Profit
4. Revenue CAGR (3yr)
5. Order Book / Backlog
6. **Gross Debt** ← REQUIRED
7. **Net Cash / Net Debt** ← REQUIRED
8. **Debt/EBITDA ratio** ← REQUIRED

## Common Errors to Avoid

1. **Using green on Recommendation slide** — Use NAVY for LOW, not green
2. **Missing risk gauge** — Must be on Slide 2
3. **Wrong logo placement** — White box backing required
4. **3-column metrics** — Use 2 columns only
5. **Missing Manu Forti logo** — Every slide, bottom-right

## When Data is Limited

**Option A: Full Product 1** — Only if 3+ years financials available
**Option B: Product 1 Lite** — Acknowledge gaps, focus on available data
**Option C: Research Memo** — Skip deck, provide structured findings

**Ask user preference when:**
- Revenue < $100M
- No published annual reports
- Recent acquisition/ownership change
- < 3 years of financial history
