# Manu Forti Sales Automation Research — Update

**Date:** 2026-03-30  
**Research Focus:** LinkedIn Outreach Automation, AI Sales Assistants, B2B Lead Generation  
**Previous Research:** 2026-03-29 (Apollo.io, Instantly.ai, n8n)

---

## 1. LINKEDIN OUTREACH AUTOMATION

### Expandi
- **Link:** https://expandi.io/pricing/
- **Use Case:** Cloud-based LinkedIn automation with smart limits to avoid bans; good for agency/multiple accounts
- **Free Trial:** 14-day full access (some sources say 7-day)
- **Pricing:** ~$99/month per seat
- **Note:** Safer than desktop tools (doesn't require 24/7 PC running)

### Lemlist
- **Link:** https://www.lemlist.com/pricing
- **Use Case:** Multi-channel outreach (email + LinkedIn + calls) with personalization at scale
- **Free Trial:** 14 days, no credit card
- **Pricing:** From ~$59/month
- **Note:** Started as email tool, now full sales engagement platform

### LinkedHelper (Budget Option)
- **Link:** https://linkedhelper.com/
- **Use Case:** Desktop LinkedIn automation at lowest cost; requires PC running 24/7
- **Pricing:** Under $180/year (~$15/month)
- **Note:** Higher ban risk than cloud solutions; best for tight budgets

## 2. AI SALES ASSISTANTS & CHATBOTS

### 11x.ai (Alice - AI SDR)
- **Link:** https://www.11x.ai/worker/alice
- **Use Case:** Fully autonomous AI SDR that researches prospects, writes personalized emails, and books meetings
- **Pricing:** ~$2,000-3,000/month (replaces human SDR)
- **Note:** Premium option; for when you're ready to scale aggressively

### Regie.ai
- **Link:** https://www.regie.ai/pricing
- **Use Case:** AI Sales Engagement Platform (SEP) with AI-generated sequences, dialer, and prospecting
- **Free Tier:** Available (limited)
- **Pricing:** AI SEP at $180/user/month; Force Multiplier at $499/user/month
- **Note:** Good middle ground between basic tools and full AI SDR

### Salesforge.ai (Agent Frank)
- **Link:** https://www.salesforge.ai/
- **Use Case:** AI SDR alternative to 11x.ai at lower price point; includes co-pilot mode for human oversight
- **Pricing:** $499/month for 1,000 active contacts
- **Note:** More affordable than 11x.ai; good entry point for AI SDR testing

## 3. B2B LEAD GENERATION (Budget-Friendly)

### Hunter.io
- **Link:** https://hunter.io/pricing
- **Use Case:** Find verified email addresses by domain or name; good for procurement leader targeting
- **Free Tier:** 25 searches/month + 50 verifications
- **Pricing:** Starter at $34/month (annual) or $49/month
- **Note:** Simple, reliable, pay-as-you-go friendly

### Lusha
- **Link:** https://www.lusha.com/pricing
- **Use Case:** Contact intelligence with direct dials and mobile numbers for procurement executives
- **Free Tier:** 5 credits/month
- **Pricing:** From $29/month
- **Note:** Strong mobile number coverage; good for phone-heavy outreach

### Seamless.AI
- **Link:** https://seamless.ai/
- **Use Case:** Real-time B2B contact search with AI verification; focuses on direct dials
- **Free Tier:** 50 credits
- **Pricing:** Custom (enterprise-focused)
- **Note:** Good data accuracy; higher price point

---

## 4. OPEN SOURCE ALTERNATIVES

### Listmonk
- **Link:** https://listmonk.app/
- **Use Case:** Self-hosted newsletter + email campaign manager; good for nurture sequences
- **Cost:** Free (self-hosted)
- **License:** AGPL-3.0

### Mautic
- **Link:** https://www.mautic.org/
- **Use Case:** Full marketing automation platform (HubSpot/Marketo alternative); self-hosted
- **Cost:** Free (self-hosted)
- **Note:** Steeper learning curve but full data control

## RECOMMENDED STACK FOR MANU FORTI

**Phase 0 (Validation - $0):**
- Apollo.io free tier (50 credits/month)
- Hunter.io free tier (25 searches/month)
- n8n self-hosted for basic automation

**Phase 1 (Soft Launch - ~$100/mo):**
- Apollo.io paid ($59/mo) — 1,000+ credits
- Instantly.ai ($37/mo) — unlimited email
- Hunter.io ($34/mo) — 500 searches

**Phase 2 (Scale - ~$500/mo):**
- Add Lemlist or Expandi for LinkedIn
- Consider Regie.ai for AI sequences

**Phase 3 (Aggressive Growth - $2K+/mo):**
- 11x.ai or Salesforge.ai for autonomous SDR
- Full sales team augmentation

## PROCUREMENT-SPECIFIC TACTICS

1. **LinkedIn Boolean Search:** `("Procurement Director" OR "Chief Procurement Officer" OR "VP Procurement") AND ("Renewable Energy" OR "Utilities" OR "Energy")`

2. **Apollo Filters:** Industry = Oil & Energy, Utilities; Title = Procurement, Sourcing, Supply Chain; Company Size = 200-5,000

3. **Email Personalization Hook:** Reference recent RFPs, sustainability reports, or expansion news
